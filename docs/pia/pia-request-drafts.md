# Open-Records Request Drafts — Certified Appraisal Roll Exports

Five counties don't post their bulk appraisal roll online, so each needs a
one-time Public Information Act request. **Send each email below from your own
email address** (requests should come from you, not an automated system).
They're written to get the electronic export delivered free or at minimal
cost, with a written estimate required before any charges.

## 1. Bexar — to: OpenRecords@bcad.org

**Subject: Public Information Request — 2025 Certified Appraisal Roll (Electronic Export)**

To the Public Information Officer, Bexar Appraisal District:

Under the Texas Public Information Act (Government Code Chapter 552), I
request a copy of the District's most recent certified appraisal roll
(tax year 2025) as an electronic data export — the standard "appraisal
data export" or "property data export" product referenced on your public
information request form.

Specifically, I request the export including, for each property account:
property ID, situs address, legal description, owner name and mailing
address, market/appraised/assessed values, exemption codes and amounts,
and the taxing entities associated with each account with taxable value
per entity. Please also include the record layout or data dictionary for
the export files.

I understand the District may offer recent data products through its FTP
server; electronic delivery by FTP, download link, or email is preferred.
If any charges apply, please provide a written itemized cost estimate
before fulfilling the request, as required by Government Code §552.2615
for charges exceeding $40.

Thank you for your time and assistance.

Jeremy Cobb
Magnolia Realty San Antonio Hill Country
jeremycobb@magnoliarealty.com
